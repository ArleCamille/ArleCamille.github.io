Summary of the Progress

During the internship, I worked on tvcast (currently NAVER TV), which is a Korean website focused on serving corporates' promotional videos. Based on YouTube at that time, I was to introduce the "watch later" feature to tvcast. The update consisted of RDBMS schema update, backend update and frontend update, which required appropriate tech stacks for each. On the final week, I finished the work by presenting the resultant feature with the test build.

During the internship, I learned how a real corporate would systematically develop a service. Also, I aimed to be a full-stack engineer not to be tied at one tech stack. Moreover, although I don't currently act as a project planner, I also participated on project planning of that feature.
